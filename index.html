What is FarmIQ?

Futuristic Agriculture Resource Management and Intelligent Quantification

Futuristic Agriculture

Modern and forward-looking farming practices using emerging technologies to increase productivity, reduce waste, and make farming more sustainable and resilient.

  • Artificial Intelligence (AI)
  • Internet of Things (IoT) sensors
  • Drones and smart machinery
  • Data analytics and automation
  • Sustainable and climate-smart methods

Resource Management

Optimizing the use of agricultural inputs and assets to maximize output while minimizing waste and cost.

  • Water (smart irrigation systems)
  • Soil nutrients and fertilizers (precision application)
  • Energy (solar-powered machinery)
  • Labor and time (automated monitoring)
  • Financial resources (data-driven budgeting)

Intelligent Quantification

Using AI, sensors, and data analysis to measure farm conditions, analyze patterns, and predict outcomes for evidence-based farming decisions.

  • Measure and monitor farm conditions
  • Analyze patterns for better decisions
  • Predict yield, pest risks, and harvest times
  • Turn raw data into intelligent insights
  • Guide farmers in smart, evidence-based actions

Development Timeline

Our roadmap for building the perfect rice farming AI solution

Current Phase
Active Development

Rice Crop Research & Data Collection

Gathering comprehensive data on Philippine rice cultivation practices, climate patterns, soil types, and common challenges.

Next 3 Months

Rice-Specific AI Model Development

Building specialized AI models for rice disease detection, irrigation optimization, yield prediction, and pest management tailored to Philippine conditions.

Months 3-5

IoT Development & Drone Integration

Developing IoT sensors for field monitoring and integrating drone technology for aerial crop assessment and data collection.

Months 6-8

Platform Development & Initial Testing

Creating the Philippine-specific farmer dashboard and conducting initial field tests with rice farmers.

Months 9-11

Pilot Program Launch

Deploying FarmIQ to selected rice farms across major rice-producing regions for real-world testing and feedback collection.

Months 12-14

Refinement & Scale Preparation

Incorporating farmer feedback, optimizing performance, and preparing for wider deployment across the Philippine rice farming community.

Specific Features

Designed exclusively for Philippine rice farming challenges

In Development

Rice Disease Detection

AI-powered identification of common Philippine rice diseases like blast, sheath blight, and bacterial leaf blight with treatment recommendations.

Planned

Smart Irrigation for Rice

Optimized water management for both irrigated and rainfed rice systems, considering Philippine weather patterns and water availability.

Planned

IoT Monitoring

Real-time field monitoring using IoT sensors for soil moisture, temperature, humidity, and crop health indicators.

Planned

Yield Prediction

Accurate yield forecasting based on variety, weather conditions, soil health, and farming practices specific to Philippine rice.

Planned

Weather Monitoring

Integration with weather APIs to provide real-time forecasts and alerts for optimal farming decisions based on local conditions.

Planned

Filipino Farmer Dashboard

User-friendly interface in Filipino and English language, designed for Philippine farming contexts.

Our Technology Approach

How FarmIQ applies advanced technology to rice farming

AI-Powered Insights

Machine learning algorithms trained specifically on Philippine rice data to provide accurate predictions and recommendations for local farming conditions.

Smart Monitoring

IoT sensors and satellite imagery to monitor crop health, soil conditions, and environmental factors in real-time across rice fields.

Data-Driven Decisions

Transforming traditional rice farming with evidence-based recommendations for planting, fertilizing, irrigating, and harvesting.
